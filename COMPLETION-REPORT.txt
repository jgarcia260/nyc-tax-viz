================================================================================
KEY INSIGHTS FROM VISUALIZATIONS
================================================================================

Revenue Comparison:
- Billionaire tax: Faster implementation but declining revenue (migration risk)
- Corporate tax: Slower implementation but growing revenue (more stable)
- Neither alone covers all needs (~10-15% of $25B total)

Borough Impact:
- Manhattan: 60% of billionaire tax revenue (high-earner concentration)
- Bronx: Only 5% (lowest concentration)
- Priorities vary by borough (Manhattan: housing, Bronx: education)

Funding Gap:
- Total improvement needs: $25B over 5 years
- Single policy revenue: $1.8-3.8B
- Gap: 85-90% unfunded
- Implication: Need hybrid approach or prioritization
